Spider-UK, also known as Billy Braddock, is a member of the Captain Britain Corps and the Spider-Army. Coming from an alternate United Kingdom in the Spider-Verse, Billy has both the powers of Spider-Man and the responsibilities of a Captain Britain-like hero.

He joins other Spider-heroes to defend the multiverse during the Spider-Verse storyline. His character blends British heritage with the web-slinging abilities of Spider-Man.
